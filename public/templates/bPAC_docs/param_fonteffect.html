enum bpac::FontEffect

Font effects

Value of enumerator:
bfeNoEffects  (0x0) No effects

bfeShadowLight  (0x1) Shadow effect 1

bfeShadow  (0x2) Shadow effect 2

bfeHorizontal  (0x3) Striping

bfeOutline  (0x4) Outline

bfeSurround  (0x5) Border

bfeFrameOut  (0x6) Framing

bfeInvertTextColors  (0x7) Invert text colors
